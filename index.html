About My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Kohei Horikoshi. Set in a world where nearly everyone possesses some superpower, called a "quirk," the story follows Izuku Midoriya, a young boy born without a quirk, who dreams of becoming a hero. After a chance encounter with the world’s greatest hero, All Might, Midoriya gains the ability to inherit his powers and begins his journey to attend U.A. High School, a prestigious academy for heroes in training.

Characters

Izuku Midoriya

Our main protagonist, also known as "Deku." Though born without a quirk, he inherits the power of One For All from All Might and works hard to prove himself as a hero.

Katsuki Bakugo

Midoriya's childhood friend and rival. Bakugo has the Explosion quirk, allowing him to create explosions with his sweat, and is highly competitive and determined.

All Might

The former No. 1 hero and Midoriya’s mentor. All Might is the Symbol of Peace and passes on his powers to Midoriya, who he sees as a worthy successor.
